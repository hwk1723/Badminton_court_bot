Booking badminton courts is always a hassle in Singapore… With the first-come-first-serve system, grabbing your preferred slot often feels like a race against time, leaving many frustrated or settling for inconvenient timings.

My AI-powered Telegram bot takes the stress out of court booking. It actively scouts Telegram groups where users release their badminton court sessions and instantly identifies potential slots that match your preferred timings. Using the power of OpenAI’s ChatGPT and the LangChain framework, the bot doesn’t just notify you—it proactively contacts the person letting go of the court, discusses availability, and confirms the booking on your behalf.

No more frantic refreshing or missed opportunities. With this bot, you get a smart, reliable, and seamless way to secure your ideal badminton court every time.
